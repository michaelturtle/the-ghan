Episode One

In this episode, I'll introduce you to The Ghan and show you some of the life on the train. We'll meet some of the staff and begin the journey from Darwin.

Episode Two

It's now time for our first stop. Come with me on one of the stops - to Nitmiluk Gorge near Katherine in the Northern Territory, and hear from a local indigenous guide.

Episode Three

This episode, I'll introduce you to some of the people who keep The Ghan running for four days as it makes its way through 3000 kilometres of harsh Australian terrain.

Episode Four

The stops each day on The Ghan take you to some iconic Australian landmarks in the outback. In this episode, I'll take you up close to the most famous of them all - Uluru.

Episode Five

Come with me into the kitchen on board The Ghan to find out how you feed 300 people gourmet meals for four days while travelling through the Australian outback.

Episode Six

We are in the middle of the South Australia desert, heading to a town where the population lives underground. This episode, I'm going to take you to Coober Pedy.

Episode Seven

On the final episode of this podcast series, we meet a man who has been working on the railways for 50 years and hear some of his stories from the tracks.
